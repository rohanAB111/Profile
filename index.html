Subject Overview

Digital System Design is a core subject for Second Year Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering students. It covers number systems, logic gates, combinational & sequential circuits, memory elements, and VHDL programming—forming the basis for embedded systems & VLSI.

About the Faculty

Dr. Rohan Appasaheb Borgalli specializes in digital systems, embedded systems, and VLSI, combining theory with hands-on examples.

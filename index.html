Subject Overview

Digital System Design is a core subject for Second Year Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering students. It includes foundational digital electronics concepts: number systems, logic gates, combinational and sequential circuits, memory elements, and programming in VHDL. This course builds the foundation for advanced topics in embedded systems and VLSI design.

About the Faculty

Dr. Rohan Appasaheb Borgalli is an experienced educator specializing in digital systems, embedded systems, and VLSI. His teaching integrates conceptual depth with practical understanding, and his lectures are ideal for academic and self-paced learning environments.
